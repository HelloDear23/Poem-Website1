Haikus


Labyrinth
The world is so cruel.
Had the minotaur wept too?
Left alone again.

Echoes
My laughter echoes.
The old house sings with my breath.
I miss being eight.

Dreams
In their sleep, dogs run.
What if I am dreaming too?
Running forever

Creativity
My marker ran dry
I drew thousands of pictures.
My eyes are still wet...

Car crash Black ice shines so nice;
we are suspended in time.
The car flipped thrice

Cuts
Snow falls around me
The red stripes make candy canes
It tastes metallic.
