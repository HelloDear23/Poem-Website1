It shall be better

In your hand, you hold a knife
It’s light and sharp, and under the lamp it gleams.
In your hand, you hold your life.
Distantly, you can hear your mother's screams.

Wouldn’t it be so nice
To stop the yelling and finally calm down?
Don’t you feel the breath of death’s ice?
Dressed up in your favorite gown.

They abandoned you, left you to rot
In a house of slamming doors.
Did you hear the shot?
Or did you just see the blood staining the floors?

You will be free, off to a better place.
You’re in the ground now, worms eating your face.
